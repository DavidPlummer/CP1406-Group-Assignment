CP1406 - Group Assignment. Barrier reef Orchestra - Project Plan

Group: David, Jonathan, Michael, Jono

Goals

Mission Statement: The website is intended to increase member sign ups for the Barrier Reef Orchestra's audience by 25%, therefore increasing revenue and prominence; intended methods include promoting featured artist and international professionals to encourage new and existing members, and aspiring musicians to join for future concerts. .

Success Evaluation

The mission to increase membership and attendance will be measured via a few means;

  1. The Evaluation will run for a 3 month period (January - March) before the start of the new website. We will then assess the 3 month evaluation period and compare the results to determine if we have reached our goal of at least a 25% increase.
  2. New members who sign up would be asked to provide feedback about Barrier reef orchestra through the sign up form, as this will include the age of the new members so we can determine if the target age of the audience is being met (20-40 years old).

If the above results show a minimum of 25% increase in membership from the website compared to the 3 month evaluation period, the website maybe considered a success. This will also provide a number of ways to improve the website based on the feedback provided by members, eg. what influenced them in becoming a member (promotions/featured artists) so the Orchestra can utilise this information to target audiences in the future, from the success of the website to improve marketing schemes for increased revenue and luring new members and member retention.


Target Audience

The target audience is between the ages of (20-40 years old). From the client brief we have idenitified this age group as the main focus. The site has been designed with a more mature layout in mind. The philharmonic and prestigious feel of music, with professional refined and fine detailed aspects. Thus making people want to get involved or be intrigued by such a prestigious organisation and attend these events. At each identified points of interests, there are sign up buttons for potential members to register.
