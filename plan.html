Goals

Mission Statement: The website is intended to increase member sign ups for Barrier Reef Orchestra by 25%, both spectators and musicians therefore increasing revenue and notoriety; Intended methods include promoting offers/benefits to encourage new and existing members to join for future concecerts. .

Success Evaluation

The mission to increase membership and attendance will be measured via a few means;

  1. The Evaluation will run a 3 month period (January - March) before the start of the new website, We will then reference back to the 3 month period before the website to compare the results to determine if we have reached our goal of at least a 50% increase
  2. Google analytics and rollout** can be used to maintain a register of new memberships via the website. It can also advise which sign up/links they used (determining what content was on that page) so that we can use this information in future events. This will also give the number of website membership sign ups.
  3. When new members enter the business in person, they can be asked to provide feedback about Barrier reef orchestra ie. "how did you hear about us" to determine if they have visited our our website.
  4. The sign up form will include the age of the new members so we can determine if the target audience is being reached (20-40)

If the above results show a minimum of 50% increase in memberships from the website compared to the prevous 3 month pre website period, the website maybe considered a success. This will also provide a number of ways to improve the website based on the feedback provided by members, eg. what drew them towards becomming a member (promotions/events/concerts/competitions) so the company can utilise this information to target audiences in the future. Incorporate ongoing and monitering to evaluate the success of the website, marketing schemes ,increase revenue, generating new members and member retention.
